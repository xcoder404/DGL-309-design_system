Gift Certificates

To make a purchase or send a gift certificate, you have three easy steps:

1. Send an email to info@outbacknursery.ca, providing us with the necessary details such as recipient information, amount, and any specific instructions. You can either leave the "TO:" and "FROM:" fields blank for personalization or let us know if you'd like us to fill them in for you.


2. Follow up by sending an e-transfer to the same email address, info@outbacknursery.ca, with the chosen amount for your purchase.


3. Once we receive your e-transfer, we'll promptly send you a scanned voucher via email. If you prefer, we can send it to another email address of your choice.
