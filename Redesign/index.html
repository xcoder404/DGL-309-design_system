Outback Nursery Unveiled

Outback Nursery, situated on Vancouver Island, stands out as one of the region's largest garden centers. Covering a sprawling 6-acre property, our nursery features five expansive greenhouses, each measuring 30x170 feet, providing nearly 23,000 square feet of space. The story of Outback Nursery begins in 1998-1999 when our parents retired to Vancouver Island from Aldergrove. Their invitation for us to join them led to the acquisition of this densely wooded property. Our journey commenced with the task of clearing trees and embracing the rural lifestyle. We ventured into raising turkeys and pigs while simultaneously nurturing hedges and conifers. Initially, our presence was felt at the Farmer's Market, but as our inventory expanded, we embarked on journeys down the island to showcase our top-tier products to potential customers. Through gradual transformation, we cleared the property bit by bit, and expanded our greenhouses and irrigation zones, which now define our establishment. Originally catering primarily to wholesale and landscaping clients, our doors eventually opened to the public due to the growing interest in our nursery.
