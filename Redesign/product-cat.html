BEECH

Beech trees, also known as Fagus, are pretty famous in the tree world. You've probably seen them around because they look pretty elegant with their smooth, grayish bark and leaves that have those serrated edges. These trees are not only nice to look at but also super tough. Some of them can survive for over 300 years! People like using their wood for making furniture, floors, and all sorts of crafty stuff.

But here's the cool part: beech trees are not just about looks and wood. They also provide homes and food for lots of animals and help keep our ecosystems healthy. So, basically, beech trees are like the wise old sages of the forest, standing tall and strong, making the world a better place.
